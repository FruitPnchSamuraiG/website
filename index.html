I'm interested in understanding what systems built around world models will look like in the coming years, and how they will touch our lives in unthinkable ways. Language was the first world these models learned to inhabit; I think and hope the ones that learn to imagine and act in real worlds will play a far more transformative role in our day to day lives than mere automation.

My path here was a winding one. I began in theory, raised on algorithms and complexity, where computation is something you prove things about, and have slowly drifted toward models that hold little worlds inside them, learning to render, simulate, and act rather than merely describe.

Right now I'm just trying to find where I belong.
