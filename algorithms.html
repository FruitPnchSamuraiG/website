Algorithms

In many ways, my indebtedness to Professor Subhash Khot

Honors Analysis of Algorithms - Dec 9, 2025

I took Honors Analysis of Algorithms during my first semester at NYU and completely fell in love with algorithms. Professor Subhash's teaching inspired me to immerse myself in theory; it has been an honor to have been his student.

The class shaped a lot of my aspirations. I have learned to struggle, to take bigger risks, only to realize how much more interesting life simply gets. The ethos of problem solving is timeless and the output may not be visible immediately, but somewhere down the line, as he put it, it will show.

I hold very fond memories of grinding through homeworks throughout the week, preparing for the finals, most of which I share with two of my classmates.
